Features Тордоки

We design features to help with route planning, navigation, and communication with organizers without unnecessary complexity.

🥾

Hiking

Route selection and preparation for walks, weekend hikes, and longer outdoor trips for different experience levels.

🛶

Rafting

Water-route cards, section notes, and useful details for participants and organizers.

🗺️

Map

Visual work with route points and a clear activity plan on the map.

〽️

Tracks

Prepare and review tracks to better evaluate distance, pace, and key points.

🧭

Safety

Checklists, checkpoints, and key group information to support safer preparation and activity control.

🤝

Organizers

Tools to describe activities, gather participants, and communicate clearly around the route.
